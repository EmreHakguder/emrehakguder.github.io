About Emre Hakgüder

Emre Hakgüder grew up in Bursa, Turkey. During his college education at Bahçesehir University in Istanbul, where he studied Advertising, he moved to Vilnius, Lithuania as an exchange student for one year. Following his graduation, he moved to Florence, Italy for his graduate education in Graphic Design. Upon returning to Turkey, he worked with many design studios and architecture firms as a graphic designer and a 3D architectural visualization artist. In 2011, he decided to pursue a graduate degree in Linguistics, his childhood dream, and started school again at Bogaziçi University in Istanbul while continuing his design work. He was introduced to Turkish Sign Language there and wrote his master's thesis on its snytax. In 2015, he started his doctoral studies at the University of Chicago Linguistics Program. He currently resides in West Loop, Chicago.
